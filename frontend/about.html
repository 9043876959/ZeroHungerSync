About ZeroHungerSync

A platform built to ensure no meal goes to waste while someone goes hungry.

🌍

The Problem We're Solving

Every year, one-third of all food produced globally is wasted — approximately 1.3 billion tonnes. Simultaneously, over 800 million people go to bed hungry every night. This paradox is one of the most critical humanitarian challenges of our time.

ZeroHungerSync was created to bridge this gap using technology, real-time coordination, and community-driven action.

How We Reduce Food Waste

ZeroHungerSync provides a real-time coordination layer between food donors (restaurants, households, caterers, farms) and NGOs who distribute food to those in need.

  • 📍 Location-based matching ensures food reaches the nearest NGO
  • ⏰ Expiry tracking prevents collection of unsafe food
  • 🔒 Lock-based acceptance prevents duplicate pickups
  • 🔔 Instant notifications ensure rapid response
  • 📊 Full audit trails for accountability
🤝
